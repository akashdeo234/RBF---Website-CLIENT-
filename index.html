Why choose us

High Quality

At RBF our quality is our identity. Our experience, precision engineering and our standards of mixing and training of our staffs and associates help to develop blocks that can stand the test of time and assure peace of mind for our clients. Our values and pursuit for excellence ensure we consistently deliver the best products for our clients.

CONTACT

Value for Money

RBF blocks are manufactured with end user in mind, this ensures that in comparison to quality traditional brick work or block work, our blocks deliver more value for money on a per square feet value of construction. RBF’s easy to deploy blocks saves direct cost by reducing the cement, water consumption, construction time and optimizes labor cost of construction through easy to build stackable design.

Sustainable

We use less water and ensure our manufacturing processes do not put undue pressure on our planet. Our bricks are engineered to last a lifetime and can be effectively re-used by carefully unstacking them during decommissioning of a construction. RBF’s also believes in entrepreneurship and strives to create job opportunities in this domain through our business partnership engagements.
